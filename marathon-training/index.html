Key principles

Weight loss first (weeks 1–12)
Easy runs at 60–70% max HR maximize fat oxidation. Lifting raises resting metabolic rate. At 215 lbs you'll burn 600–900 kcal per long run.
10% weekly mileage rule
Never increase weekly running distance by more than 10%. Cutback weeks every 4th week drop to 70%.
Lifting tapers with mileage
Weeks 1–12: 2 sessions/week. Weeks 13–23: 1 session. Weeks 24–26: zero — preserve legs for race day.
Recommended equipment
Pull-up/dip bar (~$100 Canadian Tire) and resistance bands (~$40) fill the biggest gaps in your home gym.

Toronto routes

Humber River Trail
Flat, paved — ideal for long runs. Access from Humber Valley Park, Etobicoke. 20+ km continuous.
Martin Goodman Trail
56 km lakefront loop, minimal elevation. Great for tempo and long runs Jun–Sep.
Don Valley Trail
Gentle hills — good for strength runs phase 2+. Access from Evergreen Brick Works.
Treadmill tip
Set 1% incline to match outdoor effort. Good for Apr–May interval work.
